Privacy Policy

Effective Date: August 2, 2025
Last Updated: August 2, 2025

1. Introduction

Group Post Manager ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our Chrome extension collects, uses, and protects your information.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Premium Subscription Data Only

  • Personal Identification Information: Name and email address for premium subscription account management
  • Payment Information: Billing information and payment details for premium subscription processing

2.2 Data We Do NOT Collect

  • Facebook Posts or Content: All social media posts are processed and stored locally on your device only
  • Social Media Login Information: We do not access your Facebook passwords or login credentials
  • Private Messages: We do not collect or access private conversations
  • Personal Profile Data: We do not collect your personal profile information from social media
  • Browsing History: We do not track your browsing activities
  • Usage Analytics: We do not collect or monitor your extension usage patterns

3. How We Use Your Information

3.1 Premium Subscription Data Usage

  • Account Management: Name and email address for premium subscription account creation and management
  • Payment Processing: Billing information for premium subscription payment processing
  • Customer Support: Contact information for providing customer service and technical support
  • Service Communication: Sending subscription-related notifications and updates

3.2 Local Data Processing

  • Local Processing Only: All Facebook posts and content are processed locally on your device
  • No Data Upload: We do not upload Facebook content or user activity data to our servers
  • User Control: All content organization, filtering, and exporting happens on your device

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Storage Method

  • Premium Data: Subscription and payment information stored securely on our servers with industry-standard encryption
  • Local Facebook Content: All Facebook posts and user-generated content stored locally in your browser's IndexedDB
  • Device-Specific: Local data remains on your device and is not synchronized to our servers
  • User Control: You can delete all local data at any time using extension settings

4.2 Security Measures

  • Payment Security: All payment processing handled through secure, PCI-compliant payment processors
  • Data Encryption: Premium subscription data encrypted both in transit and at rest
  • Local Data Security: Facebook content stored locally using Chrome's built-in security features
  • No Content Transmission: Facebook posts and user content never transmitted to our servers

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

5.1 Limited Data Sharing

  • Payment Processors: Billing information shared only with secure payment processing services for subscription management
  • No Facebook Content Sharing: We never share, sell, or transfer Facebook posts or user-generated content
  • No Marketing Data: We do not share personal information with advertisers or marketing companies
  • No Analytics Sharing: We do not provide user data to third-party analytics services

5.2 Legal Requirements

We may disclose premium subscription information only if required by law or in response to valid legal requests. Facebook content data stored locally on your device is not accessible to us and cannot be disclosed.

6. User Rights and Controls

6.1 Premium Subscription Rights

  • Account Access: View and update your premium subscription information
  • Data Deletion: Request deletion of your premium account and associated data
  • Subscription Management: Cancel or modify your subscription at any time
  • Data Portability: Request a copy of your premium subscription data

6.2 Local Data Control

  • Full Control: Complete control over all Facebook content stored locally on your device
  • Export Data: Export your Facebook data in Excel or JSON format at any time
  • Delete Data: Remove all local data through extension settings
  • Selective Deletion: Delete individual posts or reports as needed
  • Extension Removal: Uninstalling the extension removes all local data
