Privacy Policy

Effective Date: August 2, 2025
Last Updated: August 2, 2025

1. Introduction

Group Post Manager ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our Chrome extension collects, uses, and protects your information.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Automatically Collected Data

  • Social Media Posts: Content, images, author names, timestamps, and group information from public social media platforms
  • Usage Statistics: Number of posts collected, groups visited, and extension usage patterns
  • Browser Data: Extension settings and preferences stored locally

2.2 Data We Do NOT Collect

  • Personal Login Information: We do not access your social media passwords or login credentials
  • Private Messages: We only collect public posts and do not access private conversations
  • Personal Profile Data: We do not collect your personal profile information
  • Browsing History: We do not track your general browsing activities outside of supported platforms

3. How We Use Your Information

3.1 Primary Uses

  • Content Organization: To filter, categorize, and organize collected social media posts
  • Report Generation: To create and export reports in Excel format
  • Statistics: To provide usage statistics and analytics within the extension
  • Feature Improvement: To enhance extension functionality and user experience

3.2 Data Processing Location

  • Local Storage Only: All data is processed and stored locally on your device
  • No Server Upload: We do not upload your data to external servers
  • No Third-Party Sharing: We do not share your data with third parties

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Storage Method

  • Local Browser Storage: All data is stored in your browser's local IndexedDB
  • Device-Specific: Data remains on your device and is not synchronized across devices
  • User Control: You can delete all data at any time using the "Clear Data" feature

4.2 Security Measures

  • No Data Transmission: Since data is stored locally, there is no risk of transmission interception
  • Browser Security: We rely on Chrome's built-in security features
  • No Account Required: No user accounts or authentication required

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

5.1 No Data Sharing

  • We do not sell, trade, or transfer your data to third parties
  • We do not share data with advertisers or marketing companies
  • We do not provide data to analytics services

5.2 Legal Requirements

We may disclose information only if required by law or in response to valid legal requests.

6. User Rights and Controls

6.1 Data Access

  • View Data: Access all collected data through the extension interface
  • Export Data: Export your data in Excel format at any time
  • Data Portability: Take your data with you if you uninstall the extension

6.2 Data Control

  • Delete Data: Use the "Clear Data" feature to remove all collected information
  • Selective Deletion: Delete individual posts or reports as needed
  • Extension Removal: Uninstalling the extension removes all stored data
